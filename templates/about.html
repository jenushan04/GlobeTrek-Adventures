Crafting unforgettable journeys since 2010

Born in the coastal town of Negombo, Sri Lanka — our story is one of passion for travel and dedication to our customers.

GlobeTrek Adventures was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to make world-class travel accessible to every Sri Lankan. From humble beginnings as a small two-person operation on Lewis Place, we have grown into one of the country's most trusted travel management companies, serving over 25,000 customers across the globe.

We specialise in curated tour packages — from intimate honeymoon retreats in the Maldives to grand cultural tours through Europe, Asia, and beyond. Every itinerary we design is hand-crafted by our team of experienced travel consultants who have visited every destination we offer.
